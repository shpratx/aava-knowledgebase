# FCA — Financial Conduct Authority

## Overview
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Full Name | Financial Conduct Authority |
| Jurisdiction | United Kingdom |
| Role | Conduct regulator for 50,000+ financial services firms in the UK |
| Key Rulebook | FCA Handbook (PRIN, SYSC, COBS, BCOBS, MCOB, SUP, etc.) |
| Penalties | Unlimited fines; public censure; variation/cancellation of permissions; criminal prosecution |
| Website | fca.org.uk |

## Key Principles for Businesses (PRIN 2.1)
| Principle | Title | Banking IT Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Integrity | Systems must not facilitate market abuse or fraud |
| 2 | Skill, care and diligence | Systems must be fit for purpose; adequate testing |
| 3 | Management and control | Adequate risk management; IT governance |
| 4 | Financial prudence | N/A (capital) |
| 5 | Market conduct | N/A (trading) |
| 6 | Customers' interests | Systems must treat customers fairly; no dark patterns |
| 7 | Communications with clients | Clear, fair, not misleading UI/communications |
| 8 | Conflicts of interest | Systems must manage conflicts (e.g., product recommendations) |
| 9 | Customers: relationships of trust | Suitable products; appropriate advice |
| 10 | Clients' assets | Safeguard client money and assets |
| 11 | Relations with regulators | Cooperation; regulatory reporting systems |
| 12 | Consumer Duty | Act to deliver good outcomes for retail customers |

## Consumer Duty (PS22/9) — Effective 31 July 2023
The overarching obligation: firms must act to deliver good outcomes for retail customers.

| Outcome | Description | IT Implication |
|---|---|---|
| Products and services | Products designed to meet target market needs | Product configuration; eligibility checks |
| Price and value | Fair value for customers | Fee transparency; no hidden charges in UI |
| Consumer understanding | Customers can make informed decisions | Clear UI; plain language; appropriate disclosures |
| Consumer support | Customers can pursue their interests without unreasonable barriers | Accessible support channels; easy complaint process; no friction to cancel |
