# FCA DISP — Complaints Handling

## Regulation Reference
- Regulator: FCA — DISP (Dispute Resolution: Complaints)

## Obligation
Firms must have effective complaints handling procedures. Complaints must be acknowledged promptly, investigated fairly, and resolved within 8 weeks. If unresolved, customer must be informed of right to refer to Financial Ombudsman Service (FOS).

## Technical Controls
1. Complaint intake: multiple channels (online, phone, branch, email, post)
2. Complaint tracking: unique reference; SLA tracking; status updates to customer
3. Acknowledgment: within 5 business days of receipt
4. Resolution timeline: resolve within 8 weeks; if not, issue final response letter with FOS referral rights
5. Root cause analysis: identify systemic issues from complaint patterns
6. Regulatory reporting: complaint volumes and outcomes to FCA (per SUP 16.12)
7. FOS referral: clear information about FOS provided in final response
8. Vulnerable customer handling: enhanced support for vulnerable complainants

## Acceptance Criteria
Given a customer complaint, Then it is acknowledged within 5 business days AND tracked with unique reference AND resolved within 8 weeks or final response issued with FOS referral AND root cause analysis identifies systemic issues AND complaint data is reported to FCA.
