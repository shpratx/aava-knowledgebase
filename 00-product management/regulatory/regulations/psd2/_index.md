# PSD2 — Payment Services Directive 2

## Overview
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Full Name | Directive (EU) 2015/2366 — Payment Services Directive 2 |
| Effective | 13 January 2018; SCA enforcement from 31 December 2020 |
| Jurisdiction | EU/EEA |
| Enforcing Bodies | EBA (European Banking Authority); national competent authorities |
| Penalties | Vary by member state; typically administrative fines + license risk |
| Scope | Payment service providers, banks, third-party providers (AISPs, PISPs) |

## Key Articles for Banking
| Article | Title | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Art. 97 | Strong Customer Authentication (SCA) | Critical |
| Art. 98 | Regulatory Technical Standards for SCA | Critical |
| Art. 66-67 | Access to accounts (Open Banking) | High |
| Art. 69-70 | Obligations of PSPs regarding payment instruments | High |
| Art. 73-74 | Liability for unauthorized transactions | High |
