# Equality Act 2010

## Overview
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Full Name | Equality Act 2010 |
| Jurisdiction | United Kingdom (England, Wales, Scotland; partially Northern Ireland) |
| Effective | 1 October 2010 |
| Enforcing Bodies | EHRC (Equality and Human Rights Commission); courts |
| Penalties | Court orders; damages (unlimited); injunctions |
| Scope | All service providers including financial services |

## Protected Characteristics
| Characteristic | Banking IT Relevance |
|---|---|
| Age | Age-appropriate services; no age discrimination in digital access |
| Disability | Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA); reasonable adjustments; assistive technology support |
| Gender reassignment | Correct name/title handling in systems |
| Marriage/civil partnership | Equal treatment in joint accounts, mortgages |
| Pregnancy/maternity | No discrimination in lending decisions |
| Race | No discrimination in automated decisions; bias testing for AI/ML |
| Religion/belief | Sharia-compliant product options where offered |
| Sex | No discrimination in lending; equal access |
| Sexual orientation | Equal treatment in joint accounts, insurance |

## Key Provisions for Banking IT
| Provision | Section | IT Implication |
|---|---|---|
| Direct discrimination | s13 | Systems must not treat customers less favorably based on protected characteristic |
| Indirect discrimination | s19 | Policies/systems must not disproportionately disadvantage a protected group |
| Duty to make reasonable adjustments | s20-21 | Digital services must be accessible; alternative formats available |
| Discrimination in services | s29 | Banking services must be provided without discrimination |
| Positive action | s158 | Permitted to take steps to address disadvantage |
