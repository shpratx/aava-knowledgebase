# BCOBS — Banking Conduct of Business Sourcebook

## Overview
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Full Name | Banking: Conduct of Business Sourcebook |
| Part of | FCA Handbook |
| Jurisdiction | United Kingdom |
| Enforcing Body | FCA |
| Scope | Retail banking services: current accounts, savings accounts, cash ISAs, basic bank accounts |
| Penalties | FCA enforcement (unlimited fines, permission variation) |

## Key Chapters for Banking IT
| Chapter | Title | IT Implication |
|---|---|---|
| BCOBS 2 | Communications with banking customers | Clear, fair, not misleading UI and communications |
| BCOBS 3 | Distance communications | Online/mobile banking disclosures |
| BCOBS 4 | Information to be communicated to banking customers | Pre-contract info; terms changes; annual summaries |
| BCOBS 5 | Post-sale requirements | Statements; dormant accounts; switching |
| BCOBS 6 | Cancellation | 14-day cancellation right for distance contracts |
| BCOBS 7 | Banking customers in financial difficulty | Forbearance; signposting to debt advice |
