# Basel III — Basel Committee on Banking Supervision

## Overview
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Full Name | Basel III: International Regulatory Framework for Banks |
| Key Document | BCBS 239 — Principles for Effective Risk Data Aggregation and Risk Reporting |
| Effective | Phased implementation; BCBS 239 effective January 2016 |
| Jurisdiction | Global (implemented via national regulation) |
| Enforcing Bodies | National banking regulators (Fed, PRA, ECB, BaFin, etc.) |
| Scope | Global systemically important banks (G-SIBs); recommended for all banks |
