# SOX — Sarbanes-Oxley Act

## Overview
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Full Name | Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 |
| Effective | 30 July 2002 |
| Jurisdiction | US (applies to all US-listed companies and their subsidiaries) |
| Enforcing Body | SEC (Securities and Exchange Commission); PCAOB |
| Penalties | Criminal: up to $5M fine + 20 years imprisonment; Civil: SEC enforcement actions |
| Scope | Financial reporting, internal controls, audit trails |

## Key Sections for Banking IT
| Section | Title | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Sec 302 | Corporate Responsibility for Financial Reports | Critical |
| Sec 404 | Management Assessment of Internal Controls | Critical |
| Sec 409 | Real-Time Issuer Disclosures | High |
| Sec 802 | Criminal Penalties for Altering Documents | Critical |
