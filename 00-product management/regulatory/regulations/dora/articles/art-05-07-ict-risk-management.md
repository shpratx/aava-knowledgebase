# DORA Articles 5-7 — ICT Risk Management Framework

## Regulation Reference
- Regulation: DORA (2022/2554) — Articles 5-7

## Obligation
Financial entities must have a comprehensive ICT risk management framework approved by the management body. The framework must include strategies, policies, procedures, and tools to protect all ICT assets and information.

## Technical Controls
1. ICT asset inventory: all hardware, software, data, and network components cataloged
2. ICT risk assessment: identify threats, vulnerabilities, and impacts; updated annually and after significant changes
3. ICT security policies: access control, encryption, network security, vulnerability management, logging
4. ICT risk appetite: defined and approved by management body
5. ICT risk reporting: regular reporting to management body on ICT risk posture
6. Dedicated ICT risk management function: independent from ICT operations
7. Annual review of ICT risk management framework by management body

## Acceptance Criteria
Given a financial entity's ICT environment, Then a comprehensive ICT asset inventory exists AND ICT risks are assessed annually AND security policies cover access, encryption, network, vulnerabilities, logging AND management body approves the framework annually AND ICT risk is reported regularly to management.
