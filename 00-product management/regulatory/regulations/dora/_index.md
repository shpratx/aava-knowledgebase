# DORA — Digital Operational Resilience Act

## Overview
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Full Name | Regulation (EU) 2022/2554 — Digital Operational Resilience Act |
| Effective | 17 January 2025 |
| Jurisdiction | EU/EEA |
| Enforcing Bodies | ESAs (EBA, ESMA, EIOPA); national competent authorities |
| Penalties | Vary by member state; administrative fines + periodic penalty payments |
| Scope | Financial entities (banks, insurers, investment firms) and their ICT third-party service providers |
