# AML Directive — Anti-Money Laundering Directive

## Overview
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Full Name | Directive (EU) 2015/849 (4th AML Directive) as amended by (EU) 2018/843 (5th AML Directive) |
| Effective | 4AMLD: 26 June 2017; 5AMLD: 10 January 2020 |
| Jurisdiction | EU/EEA (transposed into national law) |
| Enforcing Bodies | National Financial Intelligence Units (FIUs); banking regulators |
| Penalties | Vary by member state; typically significant fines + license risk |
| Scope | Credit institutions, financial institutions, designated non-financial businesses |

## Key Articles for Banking
| Article | Title | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Art. 8 | Risk assessment | Critical |
| Art. 11-14 | Customer Due Diligence (CDD) | Critical |
| Art. 18-20 | Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) | Critical |
| Art. 33 | Suspicious transaction reporting | Critical |
| Art. 40 | Record retention (5 years) | Critical |
| Art. 45-46 | Third-country policies | High |
