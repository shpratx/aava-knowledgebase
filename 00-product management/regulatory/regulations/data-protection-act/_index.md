# Data Protection Act 2018 (DPA 2018)

## Overview
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Full Name | Data Protection Act 2018 |
| Jurisdiction | United Kingdom |
| Effective | 25 May 2018 (aligned with GDPR application date) |
| Enforcing Body | ICO (Information Commissioner's Office) |
| Maximum Penalty | £17.5M or 4% of annual global turnover (whichever is higher) |
| Scope | All processing of personal data in the UK |
| Relationship to GDPR | Supplements and tailors UK GDPR (retained EU law) for UK context |

## Relationship to UK GDPR
The DPA 2018 works alongside the UK GDPR (the retained EU GDPR as amended for UK context post-Brexit):
- UK GDPR provides the core data protection framework (equivalent to EU GDPR)
- DPA 2018 supplements UK GDPR with UK-specific provisions
- Together they form the UK's complete data protection regime

## Key DPA 2018 Provisions Beyond UK GDPR
| Part/Section | Title | Banking Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| Part 2 Ch 2 | Lawful bases — applied provisions | UK-specific lawful basis clarifications |
| Part 2 Ch 3 | Exemptions | National security, crime prevention, regulatory functions |
| Part 3 | Law enforcement processing | Fraud investigation data sharing |
| Part 4 | Intelligence services processing | N/A for most banking |
| s170 | Unlawful obtaining of personal data | Criminal offense — staff training required |
| s171 | Re-identification of de-identified data | Criminal offense — relevant to anonymization |

## UK-Specific Exemptions Relevant to Banking
| Exemption | DPA 2018 Reference | Banking Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| Crime prevention/detection | Sch 2 Para 2 | Sharing data with law enforcement for fraud/AML without data subject consent |
| Regulatory functions | Sch 2 Para 7 | Sharing data with FCA/PRA without data subject consent |
| Legal proceedings | Sch 2 Para 5 | Retaining data for potential legal claims |
| Management forecasting | Sch 2 Para 22 | Using customer data for business planning (with safeguards) |
| Confidential references | Sch 2 Para 24 | Employment references (HR banking) |

## ICO Enforcement
| Action | Description |
|---|---|
| Information notice | Require information from controller/processor |
| Assessment notice | Conduct audit of data processing |
| Enforcement notice | Require specific actions to comply |
| Penalty notice | Financial penalty up to £17.5M or 4% turnover |
| Criminal prosecution | For s170 (unlawful obtaining) and s171 (re-identification) offenses |
