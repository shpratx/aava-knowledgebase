# Regulatory Glossary — Standardized Terms

| Term | Definition | Regulations |
|---|---|---|
| Personal Data | Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person | GDPR Art. 4(1) |
| Data Subject | The individual whose personal data is processed | GDPR Art. 4(1) |
| Data Controller | Entity that determines purposes and means of processing | GDPR Art. 4(7) |
| Data Processor | Entity that processes data on behalf of the controller | GDPR Art. 4(8) |
| PII | Personally Identifiable Information — equivalent to GDPR "personal data" | General |
| PAN | Primary Account Number — the card number | PCI-DSS |
| CDE | Cardholder Data Environment — systems storing/processing/transmitting card data | PCI-DSS |
| SCA | Strong Customer Authentication — two-factor auth for payments | PSD2 Art. 97 |
| CDD | Customer Due Diligence — identity verification for AML | AML Directive Art. 11 |
| EDD | Enhanced Due Diligence — additional checks for high-risk customers | AML Directive Art. 18 |
| KYC | Know Your Customer — process of verifying customer identity | AML Directive |
| SAR | Suspicious Activity Report — filed with FIU for suspicious transactions | AML Directive Art. 33 |
| CTR | Currency Transaction Report — filed for transactions >= threshold | BSA/AML |
| FIU | Financial Intelligence Unit — national authority receiving SARs | AML Directive |
| PEP | Politically Exposed Person — requires enhanced due diligence | AML Directive Art. 20 |
| DPIA | Data Protection Impact Assessment — risk assessment for high-risk processing | GDPR Art. 35 |
| DPO | Data Protection Officer — designated privacy officer | GDPR Art. 37 |
| DPA | Data Protection Authority — national supervisory authority | GDPR |
| SCCs | Standard Contractual Clauses — mechanism for cross-border data transfers | GDPR Art. 46 |
| BCRs | Binding Corporate Rules — intra-group transfer mechanism | GDPR Art. 47 |
| TIA | Transfer Impact Assessment — assess destination country data protection | GDPR (EDPB guidance) |
| ICFR | Internal Controls over Financial Reporting | SOX Sec 404 |
| PCAOB | Public Company Accounting Oversight Board | SOX |
| RTO | Recovery Time Objective — max acceptable downtime | DORA Art. 11 |
| RPO | Recovery Point Objective — max acceptable data loss | DORA Art. 11 |
| ICT | Information and Communication Technology | DORA |
| G-SIB | Global Systemically Important Bank | Basel III |
| BCBS | Basel Committee on Banking Supervision | Basel III |
| TDE | Transparent Data Encryption — database-level encryption | PCI-DSS, GDPR Art. 32 |
| HSM | Hardware Security Module — secure key management device | PCI-DSS Req 3.6 |
| WAF | Web Application Firewall | PCI-DSS Req 6 |
| mTLS | Mutual TLS — bidirectional certificate authentication | DORA Art. 9, PCI-DSS |
